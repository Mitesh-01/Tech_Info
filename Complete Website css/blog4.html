Internet of Things (IoT)

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to a network of interconnected physical devices, vehicles, appliances, sensors, and other objects embedded with sensors, software, and connectivity, enabling them to collect and exchange data over the internet. Essentially, IoT allows objects to be controlled, monitored, and interacted with remotely across a network infrastructure.
